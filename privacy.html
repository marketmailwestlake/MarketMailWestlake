Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Who We Are

MarketMail Westlake is a direct mail advertising service based in Austin, Texas, operated by Wyatt Harris. We connect local businesses with 15,000 affluent homeowners in the Westlake Hills area (ZIP codes 78746 and 78733) through co-op direct mail campaigns.

You can reach us at wyatt@marketmailwestlake.com or (512) 964-3682.

Information We Collect

When you submit a form on our website or through our Meta (Facebook/Instagram) advertisements, we collect the following information:

  • Your name
  • Your phone number
  • Your email address
  • Your business name and industry (if provided)

We collect this information solely to follow up with you about advertising opportunities on the MarketMail Westlake mailer.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Contact you to discuss available advertising slots
  • Send you invoices and confirm campaign details if you become a client
  • Follow up on your inquiry via phone or email

We do not use your information for any purpose other than direct communication related to MarketMail Westlake advertising services.

We Do Not Sell Your Information

We will never sell, rent, trade, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes. Your contact details are used only by MarketMail Westlake to follow up on your inquiry.

Data Storage

Your information is stored securely through our lead management tools. We retain your contact information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected. If you would like your information removed, contact us at any time and we will delete it promptly.

Meta Advertising

We run lead generation advertisements on Facebook and Instagram through Meta's advertising platform. When you submit a lead form through one of our Meta ads, your information is collected through Meta's platform and shared with us. Meta's own Privacy Policy governs their data practices. You can review Meta's Privacy Policy at facebook.com/privacy/policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request that we correct or delete your information
  • Opt out of further communication from us at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us directly.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
